Saved Information

This application does not permanently store any of your data. It does store, on your computer, your save file. We do not have access to your save file, unless you share your save file with us. (We don't recommend that)

Your save file is stored on your root folder. It is not a cookie, but it's like one. This save file expires after 3 months. The save file is stored so that when you refresh, or close the page, your achievements and other items on your save file will remain saved.


External Sources/Scripts

Unlike many other websites, we do not rely on or use Google Analytics/Ad Services. These scripts track you across websites, and "personalize" your ads, while giving infomation about you to the website owner.

We have, though, a few external script sources that help with notifications, localStorage accessibillity, and text encoding.
